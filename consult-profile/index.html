The setup spans over three data centers and is based on Red Hat Openstack Platform 13 and Red Hat Ceph Storage 3. The design includes everything from different availability zones, storage pools (both local and stretched between multiple data centers), and involves roughly 50 nodes. The deployment of OpenStack is done through the Red Hat Director, while Ceph is being deployed using the Ceph Ansible Playbook. We have chosen this configuration to get an environment that scales and fulfills our internal customers requirements.
