An Android app for parents

Barnkollen primarily targets parents who wishes to easily control which YouTube-clips their kids should be able to watch. As a parent you simply search for video clips and press the Add button for each video you want to allow.

The video will then be added to Barnkollens internal playlist and there is no way for the kid to click its way to other videos. The main idea behind Barnkollen is to allow an parent what videos that should be available for your kid - no more, no less. Another goal is that the app itself should be really easy to use, both for a parent as well as for the kids.
